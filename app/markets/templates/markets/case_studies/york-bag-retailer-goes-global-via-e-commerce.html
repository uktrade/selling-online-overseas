In 2013, Maxwell Scott Bags noticed an increase in orders from Germany. They decided to launch both a German and US website and successfully increased their exporting business.

Exporting overseas has seen the business grow by 300% in 3 years and led to the appointment of a Head of e-Marketplaces who focuses on expanding their business further in the online world.

The business asked the Department for International Trade (DIT) for marketplace advice to help increase their business. From listening to the e-commerce advisor at DIT, 25% of Maxwell Scott Bags export sales are now through Amazon.
